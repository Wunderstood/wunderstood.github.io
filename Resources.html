Questions We're Frequently Asked

1

How Do I Use This Tool?
Wunderstood seamlessly integrates with your browsing experience to quicken your investigations and research tasks. Whether you're working on a company blog, academic project, or marketing research, Wunderstood streamlines the process of collecting and managing notes from web pages. Say goodbye to time-consuming reading and note-taking, as the tool automatically detects lengthy text and reads it for you. Once you've collected your notes, you can effortlessly connect the dots between any set of notes for your research topics. Wunderstood then works its magic, generating a cohesive and insightful report, saving you valuable time and effort.

2

How Does The AI Work?
At Wunderstood, we leverage cutting-edge LLM solutions from OpenAI and other leading providers. Rest assured, your reports are stored locally, and we only access pages when you command us to do so. Your privacy and security are our top priorities. See more about our privacy policy.

3

What Do Reports Look Like?
 Our reports are constantly evolving with your valuable feedback. Currently, they encompass all the essential sections found in a comprehensive research report, such as an introduction, source selection, research methodology, findings and analysis, and detailed references. Your insights drive our continuous improvement.


4

What Are The Usage Guidelines?
 Wunderstood's AI technology is designed to assist users in their research endeavors and should be used responsibly and ethically in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. We do not condone plagiarism or copyright infringement and encourage users to respect intellectual property rights. You're encouraged to customize the generated reports to suit your specific needs and preferences.

5

Who Do I Contact If I Have Questions?
 If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to email support@wunderstood.com. We are here to help and will respond to your inquiries promptly. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we look forward to providing you with the best possible support.

6

Where Can I Access Wunderstood?
You can access Wunderstood on the Chrome Web Store. Simply visit the Chrome Web Store and search for "Wunderstood" to find our extension. Once you locate it, click on "Add to Chrome" to install Wunderstood and start experiencing the power of our AI Researching Assistance right at your fingertips.

7

Where Can I Create Reports?
Crafting reports with Wunderstood is easy and convenient. Access the Wunderstood options page directly by clicking the "Craft" button within the popup. Alternatively, right-click on the extension pin and select "Options" from the menu to navigate there.

Please note that reports generated from extensive text may take approximately 1-2 minutes to complete. Rest assured, once your report is ready, you'll receive a Chrome notification, indicating its successful creation. 

8

Where Can I Access Reports?
Your reports are securely stored locally on your browser. To access them, simply click the settings icon within the Wunderstood popup. Alternatively, right-click on the extension pin, select "Options", navigat to "Craft Reports", and click "Download Reports".
